# **Day 8 – Azure Pricing, SLAs & Support**

This document covers **cost management and support** topics.

---

# **1. Azure Pricing Concepts**

### **Factors Affecting Cost**

* Compute time
* Storage usage
* Data transfer
* Region

---

# **2. Cost Management Tools**

* **Azure Pricing Calculator** – estimate cost before deployment
* **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)** – compare on-prem vs Azure
* **Azure Cost Management** – monitor and control spend

---

# **3. Cost Optimization**

* Reserved Instances
* Auto-scaling
* Shutting down unused resources
* Right-sizing VMs

---

# **4. Service Level Agreements (SLAs)**

### **What is SLA?**

Guarantee of uptime expressed as a percentage.

### **Common SLAs**

* 99.9%
* 99.99%

### **Key Rule**

* Using multiple services does NOT combine SLAs

---

# **5. Service Lifecycle**

* Preview → General Availability → Deprecated
